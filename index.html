Common questions

I'm not getting notifications.

Make sure notifications are enabled for That Day in the iOS Settings app, under Notifications → That Day. The app will also remind you if access is turned off.

Where is my data stored?

Everything stays on your device. That Day has no accounts and no servers — your days, photos, and reminders never leave your iPhone.

Can I change the app's colors?

Yes. Open Settings inside the app to adjust the background and card colors to your taste.

How do I switch languages?

That Day is available in English and 中文. You can switch anytime from Settings inside the app.
