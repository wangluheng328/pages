Privacy Policy

Last updated June 23, 2026

The short version: That Day keeps everything on your device. We don't collect your data, because we never see it.

Information we collect

None. That Day has no user accounts and no servers of our own. The days you add, the people and photos you save, and the reminders you create are all stored locally on your device using Apple's on-device storage. This information never leaves your iPhone and is never transmitted to us or anyone else.

Photos

If you choose to add a photo to a day or a memory, the app accesses only the specific images you select from your photo library. Those photos are stored privately within the app on your device. We do not upload, share, or have any access to them.

Notifications

That Day schedules local notifications on your device to remind you of upcoming days. These reminders are generated and delivered entirely on your iPhone by iOS. No notification content is sent through any external service.

Third parties & tracking

That Day contains no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking of any kind. We do not use cookies or identifiers to follow you across apps or websites.

Data retention & deletion

Because your data lives only on your device, you are always in full control of it. Deleting a day, a memory, or the app itself permanently removes the associated data from your device.

Children's privacy

That Day does not collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date above.
